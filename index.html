Let's build a better workspace

Co-manity's mission is simple: to build a stronger community inside and outside our office walls. We consist of a small group of individuals who have the flexibilty to work from home or remote but all found ourselves wanting more out of our everyday work routine. Ideally Co-manity will be a place where people can go to get out of the house, be more productive and also have the support of all our community members. Our inagural office is only accepting a limited number of participants so if you're looking for something more please contact us and help grow a great community!
