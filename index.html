Let's build a better workspace

"Without community there is no humanity!"

Co-manity's mission is simple: to build a stronger community inside and outside our office walls. We consist of a small group of individuals who have the flexibilty to work from home or remote but all found ourselves wanting more out of our everyday work routine. Ideally Co-manity will be a co-working space where people can go to get out of the house, be more productive and also have the support of all our community members. Our inagural office is only accepting a limited number of participants so if you're looking for something more please contact us and help grow a great community!
